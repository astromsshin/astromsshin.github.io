Compilation of comoving SFR (M_sun yr^-1 Mpc^-3)

This page is for personal usages. But feel free to find useful information in this web page.

Theoretical studies are:

  • Schaye et al., MNRAS, 2009 : from the OverWhelmingly Large Simulations project
  • Choi & Nagamine, MNRAS, 393, 1595, 2009 : effects of metal enrichment and metal cooling
  • Wang et al., MNRAS, 384, 1301, 2008 : the cosmic SFR for WMAP1 and WMAP3 parameters.
  • Faucher-Giguère et al., ApJ, 688, 85, 2008 : the effect of ionizing background.
  • Hernquist & Springel, MNRAS, 341, 1253, 2003 : the change of sigma_8, omega_baryon, and metal cooling are considered.
  • Rowan-Robinson, ApJ, 549, 745, 2001 : the parametrized approach
  • Sadat et al., A&A, 369, 26, 2001 : combining a series of chemo-spectrophotometric models
  • Pei et al., ApJ, 522, 604, 1999 : analytical approach
  • Casse et al., New Astronomy, 3, 259, 1998 : the compilation of old models
